The App

SEAT is a mobile app that gives spectators the opportunity to order food and drinks in a stadium delivered straight to their seats. This will be supplemented by an alternative option to skip the queues and order food for collection. The product consists of two smart phone applications: The User App and Vendor App. The two apps necessitate each other and together provide a smooth and effective platform for both the customer and vendor.

SEAT is a unique and elegant solution to a problem that consistently irritates spectators - queuing. The SEAT app will be user friendly and aesthetically pleasing. Allowing fans to enjoy the match without a moment missed.

The SEAT delivery service will be available for any well-facilitated stadium featuring numerated seats. As well as working a stand alone app, SEAT can be integrated into outside mobile applications.
